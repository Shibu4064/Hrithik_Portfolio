01 — About

Who I Am

I'm a Computer Science Engineer and AI researcher currently completing my MSc in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex, UK. My journey spans academia and applied research — from teaching 40–60 students per class to co-authoring papers published at top-tier NLP and computer vision venues.

My research spans Natural Language Processing (low-resource languages, fake news detection, sentiment analysis), Computer Vision (medical imaging, CNNs, Vision Transformers), and Agricultural AI. I am driven by the challenge of building systems that work in resource-constrained, real-world conditions.

I hold a BSc in Computer Science & Engineering from Shahjalal University of Science & Technology (CGPA 3.61/4.00), where I also contributed to research datasets, organised international conferences, and served in multiple leadership roles.
